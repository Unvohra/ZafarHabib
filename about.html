Our History

Established in 1992, Zafar Habib Packages is a name synonymous with originality, creativity and an endeavor towards perfection. Since its inception, it has diversified its product range from manufacturing Printing & corrugated packing items. This is due to the fact that we believe in proactively identifying and fulfilling market needs without compromising on quality management. From Insert Cards, graphic hang-tags and printing solutions for various types of labels, to security products applied at the source, for Home Textiles, Garments. Zafar Habib Packages offers a world of solutions. Today we are one of the most prominent and leading name of printing & packaging manufacturers in Pakistan.
