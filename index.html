About Us

Zafar Habib Packages (Pvt.) Ltd stands as a prominent and reputable packaging company in Pakistan. Having evolved into a fully equipped printing and packaging facility, we have established ourselves as a freestanding entity in the industry. Backed by a highly skilled team and decades of experience, we offer the convenience of accessing printing, packaging, labels, and cardboard hangers all in one place. Our approachable and competent service ensures that your requirements are met efficiently and effectively, providing you with comprehensive solutions under a single roof.
